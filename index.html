Who I Am

I'm a student film maker, who loves the medium of film, and the idea of telling complex stories through film. I'm primarily a director and editor but also enjoy cinematography and cover that role when needed.

What I Do

I work on various short film and other miscellaneous projects in my free time, my proudest work are the longer short films where I'm able to tell a complex story. However, I also enjoy making supercuts, b-roll compilation and marketing material for various events. All while pursing a degree in studio art at the University of Toronto
